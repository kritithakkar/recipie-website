SANDESH

TOTAL TIME
30m
PREP TIME
10 m
CALORIES
257

Ingredients
150 gm paneer
4 crushed lightly green cardamom
6 tablespoon sugar
1/2 cup grated khoya
7 strand saffron
6 shredded almonds
Method
Step 1
Sandesh is an authentic Bengali delicacy, prepared with the goodness of kheer and cheena. Take a large bowl and add the cheena, khoya, sugar/ gur and mix all the ingredients.
sandesh.
Step 2
Blend the ingredients in to a smooth mixture. You can do this in a blender or use the backside of a bowl to mash them together. Mix in the elaichi and set in a layer about 1/2 inch thick.
 Refrigerate till set and cut into squares or diamonds and serve.
